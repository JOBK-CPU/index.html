About Me

I'm Rebecca Biwwot —
Explorer of Beautiful Africa

Born and raised in Kenya, I've always believed that Africa's greatest stories are told not in books, but on the trails, in the forests, at the summit of mountains, and in the buzzing hearts of its cities.

I started RebeccaBiwwot Explores to show the world what I see every day — a continent of breathtaking beauty, rich culture, warm people, and endless adventure. From the misty heights of Aberdare Forest to the neon lights of Nairobi's malls, every destination has changed me.

Through my blog and vlogs, I want you to feel what I feel — the cool highland breeze, the thunder of a waterfall, the hush of a wild savanna at dawn. Africa is not just a place. It is a feeling.
